American Express

Scope
Rebrand and relaunch of American Express' intranet to support the life and career of Amex Colleagues.

Deliverables
 Current state audit, trendscape, information architecture, wireframes, screen designs, style guide, digital prototype

My Role
Interaction design, design research

Team
4 designers, 1 program manager

Some background

A company defined by its culture and values has something to prove.

American Express is a leading financial services corporation specializing in payment cards. Their employees–Colleagues–know them to be a company that lives by their values, with a supportive culture and great benefits.

Their intranet–Colleague Services–holds information for colleagues of all levels, holding essential HR information, as well as general policies and documents for conducting work. Colleagues feel their systems are outdated and untrustworthy, and that makes them question whether Amex is really innovative.

How might we relaunch Colleague Services into a trusted support hub for Amex colleagues?

The full details of this case study are currently under construction.

Can't wait? Let's chat and I'll give you a sneak peak of the process.
